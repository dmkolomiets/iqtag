    How It Works

    Generate & Secure
    Upload product information, certifications, and media files through our easy-to-use platform. We generate two unique QR codes—one public and one hidden-secured by blockchain technology.
    Validate & Verify
    Customers scan the public QR code to access product details stored on the blockchain. The hidden QR code is scanned upon purchase to confirm product authenticity, automatically recording the transaction on the blockchain.
    Reward & Engage
    By scanning the hidden QR code, customers can mint an exclusive NFT linked to their purchase. This NFT can be stored, traded, or redeemed for rewards, enhancing customer loyalty and brand engagement.
